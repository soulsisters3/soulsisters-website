DALGONA COFFEE

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp instant coffee

  • 2 tbsp sugar

  • 400-500ml milk (we used whole milk)

      • Directions

    1. Whisk the coffee, sugar and 3 tbsp boiling water in a bowl for approximately 5 mins until the mixture is thick and fluffy with stiff peaks. This is easiest done using an electric whisk but can be done by hand. (If whisking by hand, it will take 8 to 12 minutes to get to optimal fluffiness.)
